# Fitinerary 🧳

Fitinerary helps you **pack smart**. Tell it where you're going and when, and it
figures out what clothes to bring — or buy before you leave — based on the weather
at your destination.

**🔗 Live website:** https://numan98khan.github.io/fitinerary/

---

## How to make changes (no coding needed!)

You don't need to know anything technical. Just open Claude in this project and
describe what you want in plain words. For example:

- *"Make the title bigger and change the color to blue."*
- *"Add a page where I can type in a city and see its weather."*
- *"I don't love the current look — make it feel more fun and colorful."*

Claude will make the change, put it online, and tell you when it's ready. After
each change, give the live website a minute or two to update, then refresh the page.

## Tips

- Be as specific or as loose as you like — *"make it prettier"* works just as well
  as a detailed request.
- If something doesn't look right, just say so and Claude will fix it.
- You can always ask *"can you explain what you changed?"* and get a simple answer.
